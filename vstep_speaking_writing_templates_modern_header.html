01 · Framework

Công thức làm bài dễ nhớ

Chỉ cần nhớ khung, sau đó thay chủ đề, lý do và ví dụ.

ARE

Speaking Part 1

Answer trực tiếp → Reason → Example. Mục tiêu: trả lời 3–5 câu rõ ý.

ICE

Speaking Part 2

Introduce choice → Compare options → End. Luôn phân tích cả 3 phương án.

TIE

Speaking Part 3 / Writing

Topic → Ideas → Example/End. Dùng 2–3 ý chính, ví dụ ngắn, kết luận rõ.

02 · Reusable templates

Template copy nhanh

Mở từng card để xem mẫu đầy đủ, bấm copy để dùng luyện đề.

Task 1#01

Email thân mật cho bạn bè

Dùng cho đề trả lời bạn, mời đi chơi, hỏi thăm, kể trải nghiệm.

Xem template 
Hi [Name],

Thank you for your email. I was very happy to hear from you. I am writing to answer your questions about [topic].

First of all, [answer point 1]. I think this is a good idea because [reason/example].

Secondly, about [point 2], I would suggest that [suggestion/advice]. In my opinion, this will help you [benefit].

Finally, regarding [point 3], I think you should / should not [answer]. The main reason is that [reason]. For example, [simple example].

I hope my information is useful for you. Please let me know if you need anything else.

Best wishes,
[Your name]
Task 1#02

Email đưa lời khuyên

Dùng cho đề bạn hỏi nên học gì, chọn gì, chuẩn bị gì.

Xem template 
Hi [Name],

Thanks for your email. I understand that you are thinking about [situation]. In my opinion, you should [main advice].

There are several reasons for my suggestion. Firstly, [reason 1]. This is important because [explanation]. Secondly, [reason 2]. It can help you [benefit]. Finally, [reason 3 or example].

However, I do not think you should [rejected option] because [reason]. It may be [problem], especially when [situation].

I hope my advice can help you make a good decision. Tell me what you decide later.

Best wishes,
[Your name]
Task 1#03

Email xin lỗi

Dùng cho đề hủy hẹn, không tham gia được, làm mất đồ, quên việc.

Xem template 
Hi [Name],

I am writing to say sorry for [problem]. I know this may cause you some trouble, and I feel really sorry about it.

The reason is that [explain reason]. I did not expect this to happen, so I could not inform you earlier.

To solve this problem, I would like to [solution]. I can also [another solution] if it is convenient for you.

Once again, I am very sorry for this situation. I hope you can understand.

Best wishes,
[Your name]
Task 1#04

Complaint letter

Dùng cho đề phàn nàn dịch vụ, sản phẩm, khách sạn, khóa học.

Xem template 
Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to complain about [product/service] that I used / bought on [time].

There are several problems that I would like to mention. Firstly, [problem 1]. Secondly, [problem 2]. In addition, [problem 3]. Because of these problems, I felt very disappointed and uncomfortable.

I would like you to [solution: give me a refund / replace the product / improve the service / contact me soon]. I believe this is a reasonable request.

I look forward to hearing from you soon.

Yours faithfully,
[Your name]
Task 2#05

Opinion / Agree or Disagree

Dùng cho đề Do you agree or disagree? To what extent do you agree?

Xem template 
Nowadays, many people believe that [statement]. In my opinion, I partly / completely agree with this view because [reason 1] and [reason 2].

Firstly, [main idea 1]. This is because [explanation]. For example, [example]. As a result, [result/benefit]. Therefore, it is clear that [link back to topic].

Secondly, [main idea 2]. In modern society, [explanation]. For instance, [example]. This can help people [benefit] and avoid [problem].

However, some people may argue that [opposite idea]. This may be true in some cases, but I believe that [your response]. The reason is that [reason].

In conclusion, I strongly believe that [repeat your opinion]. Although [opposite side], [your opinion] is more important because [final reason].
Task 2#06

Advantages and Disadvantages

Dùng cho đề technology, online learning, living abroad, social media, fame.

Xem template 
In recent years, [topic] has become more and more popular. This trend brings both advantages and disadvantages to individuals and society.

On the one hand, there are several benefits of [topic]. Firstly, [advantage 1]. This means that [explanation]. For example, [example]. Secondly, [advantage 2]. Thanks to this, people can [benefit].

On the other hand, [topic] also has some drawbacks. The first disadvantage is that [disadvantage 1]. This may lead to [negative result]. Another problem is [disadvantage 2]. For instance, [example].

In my opinion, the advantages of [topic] are greater than the disadvantages if people use it in a suitable way. It is important to [solution/advice].

In conclusion, [topic] has both positive and negative sides. However, with careful use and proper control, it can bring more benefits than problems.
Task 2#07

Discussion Essay

Dùng cho đề Some people think..., while others believe...

Xem template 
People have different opinions about [topic]. Some people believe that [view 1], while others think that [view 2]. This essay will discuss both views and give my opinion.

On the one hand, there are some reasons why people support [view 1]. Firstly, [reason 1]. This is because [explanation]. For example, [example]. Secondly, [reason 2]. Therefore, this view is understandable.

On the other hand, I believe that [view 2] is more reasonable. The first reason is that [reason 1]. In addition, [reason 2]. For instance, [example]. As a result, [positive outcome].

In my opinion, although [view 1] has some benefits, [view 2] is better because [main reason]. It is more suitable for modern life and can help people [benefit].

In conclusion, both views have their own reasons. However, I personally believe that [your opinion].
Task 2#08

Problem and Solution

Dùng cho đề pollution, stress, unemployment, obesity, traffic, social media addiction.

Xem template 
Nowadays, [problem] has become a serious issue in many countries. There are several causes of this problem, and some solutions should be taken to improve the situation.

There are two main reasons for [problem]. Firstly, [cause 1]. This happens because [explanation]. For example, [example]. Secondly, [cause 2]. As a result, [negative consequence].

To solve this problem, both individuals and the government should take action. First, [solution 1]. This can help [benefit]. Second, [solution 2]. For example, [example]. In addition, people should [personal action].

In conclusion, [problem] is mainly caused by [cause 1] and [cause 2]. However, it can be solved if [solution summary].
Speaking#09

Part 1 – A-R-E

Answer trực tiếp → Reason → Example / Extra information.

Xem template 
Personally, I would say that [answer]. The main reason is that [reason]. For example, when I [example], I usually feel [feeling/result]. That is why [repeat idea].
Speaking#10

Part 2 – I-C-E

Choose → Explain chosen option → Reject other options → Conclude.

Xem template 
In this situation, I think the best option is option [number]: [chosen option]. I choose this option for several reasons.

First of all, [chosen option] is the most suitable because [reason 1]. It can help [person] [benefit]. For example, [example].

Secondly, this option is also better because [reason 2]. If [person] chooses this option, he/she can [positive result].

Option [number] is also useful because [advantage of another option]. However, I do not think it is the best choice because [weakness].

Option [number] may be good in some cases because [advantage]. However, it has one problem: [weakness]. Therefore, it may not be very suitable for this situation.

For these reasons, I believe option [chosen number] is the best choice. It is practical, helpful, and suitable for [person/situation].
Speaking#11

Part 3 – Topic Development

Topic statement → Idea 1 → Idea 2 → Idea 3 → Conclusion.

Xem template 
Today, I would like to talk about [topic]. In my opinion, [topic] is very important in modern life for several reasons.

First of all, [idea 1]. This is important because [explanation]. For example, [example]. Therefore, [idea 1] can bring many benefits.

Secondly, [idea 2]. In daily life, people often [situation]. If they [action], they can [benefit]. This shows that [idea 2] plays an important role.

Finally, [idea 3]. I believe this is also necessary because [reason]. For instance, [example]. As a result, people can [positive result].

In conclusion, [topic] is important because of [idea 1], [idea 2], and [idea 3]. In the future, I think people should pay more attention to this issue.

SpeakingWritingThe importance of education
Speaking prompt

Do you like studying English?

Yes, I do. I like studying English because it is very useful for my study and future career. For example, I can read English documents, watch English videos, and communicate with foreigners. Although learning English is sometimes difficult, I think it gives me more opportunities in life.

Writing / Part 3 idea

The importance of education

Education plays an important role in modern society. First of all, education helps people get better jobs. If students study well, they can have more knowledge and skills for their future career. Secondly, education helps people become more confident and independent. For example, educated people can solve problems better in daily life. Finally, education can improve society because people with good education often behave more responsibly. In conclusion, education is necessary for both individuals and society.

Technology – Công nghệ

SpeakingWritingThe advantages and disadvantages of technology
Speaking prompt

Do you often use technology?

Yes, I use technology every day. The main reason is that it helps me save a lot of time. For example, I use my phone to study English, check emails, read news, and communicate with friends. However, I think people should not depend too much on technology because it can affect their health and social life.

Writing / Part 3 idea

The advantages and disadvantages of technology

Nowadays, technology has become an important part of modern life. It brings both advantages and disadvantages to people. On the one hand, technology has many benefits. Firstly, it helps people communicate more easily. Secondly, technology supports education and work. Students can learn online, while workers can complete tasks faster with computers and the Internet. On the other hand, technology also has some drawbacks. People may spend too much time on phones or computers, and face-to-face communication may become less common. In conclusion, technology is very useful, but people should use it in a balanced way.

04 · Language bank

Phrase bank dùng cho hầu hết chủ đề

Các cụm câu này có thể chèn vào Speaking Part 2/3 và Writing Task 2.

Mở bài

  • Nowadays, [topic] has become more and more important in modern society.
  • In recent years, many people have paid more attention to [topic].
  • People have different opinions about [topic].
  • There is no doubt that [topic] plays an important role in our daily lives.

Nêu lý do

  • The main reason is that...
  • This is because...
  • One possible reason is that...
  • Another important reason is that...

Nêu lợi ích

  • It helps people save time and money.
  • It improves people’s quality of life.
  • It gives people more opportunities to learn and develop themselves.
  • It helps people become more confident and independent.
  • It is useful for both study and work.

Nêu bất lợi

  • It may cause stress and pressure.
  • It can have a negative effect on people’s health.
  • People may waste too much time on it.
  • It can make people less active and less independent.
  • It may create some problems in daily life.

Nêu giải pháp

  • People should use it in a balanced way.
  • The government should introduce stricter rules.
  • Schools and families should educate young people about this issue.
  • Individuals should be more responsible for their actions.
  • It is important to raise people’s awareness.

Kết luận

  • In conclusion, I believe that this issue is important in modern society.
  • Although there are some disadvantages, the benefits are more significant.
  • For these reasons, I think this is the best choice.
  • Overall, people should use it wisely and responsibly.
05 · Study plan

Lộ trình học nhanh 10 ngày

Tập trung vào khung câu, không học thuộc máy móc.

Ngày 1–2: Học 3 công thức chính: A-R-E, I-C-E và Topic → Ideas → Conclusion.
Ngày 3–5: Ưu tiên 6 chủ đề rất hay gặp: Education, Technology, Social Media, Health, Environment, Work.
Ngày 6–8: Luyện nhóm hay gặp: Family, Friends, Travel, City Life, Transport, Online Learning.
Ngày 9: Bấm giờ Speaking: Part 2 khoảng 2 phút, Part 3 khoảng 3 phút.
Ngày 10: Bấm giờ Writing: Task 1 khoảng 20 phút, Task 2 khoảng 40 phút.
